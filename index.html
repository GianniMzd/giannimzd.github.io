Portfolio optimization · 2026
Optimal allocation finding for an aggressive portfolio under given VaR constraint
Under the assumption of economic growth, the idea is to determine the allocation that gives the max beta portfolio with a given MAVaR constraint using KDE Monte Carlo simulation, SLSQP optimization, and walk-forward backtesting with train/deploy split.

Accounting · 2026
Airline reporting comparison regarding IFRS 16 and how does Air France manage to bypass this standard.
We compare the implications of the new IFRS 16 (leasing) standard for airlines and highlight the accounting strategies used to mitigate its impact. We also analyze the case of Air France, which took advantage of the "financing lease" to lower its lease liability.

Risk Management · 2026
Using Kernel Density Function and Student's t-distribution to model Value at Risk and Expected Shortfall using Monte Carlo method
Co-authored preprint with Yuto Murata. Monte Carlo method used on non-parametric KDE and parametric Student's t-distribution to model Value at Risk and Expected Shortfall and compare it to traditional GBM. It takes into consideration the Basel III/FRTB framework and uses a backtesting methodology to evaluate the performance of the model.
